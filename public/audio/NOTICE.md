# Audio credits — Mundial 2026 traditions section

- **cielito-lindo.ogg** — *Cielito Lindo* (Quirino Mendoza y Cortés, 1882).
  Song in the public domain. Instrumental synthesizer rendition sourced from
  [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Anonimo_-_Cielito_lindo.ogg)
  under a Public Domain dedication by the uploader.

- **trompeta-fanfare.wav** — Synthetic trumpet fanfare generated by
  `scripts/gen-audio.py` (additive synthesis, ADSR envelope).
  Released by the project under **CC0 1.0 Universal** (public domain).

- **multitud-ola.wav** — Synthetic stadium-crowd "olé" ambience generated by
  `scripts/gen-audio.py` (filtered pink noise with crescendo peaks).
  Released by the project under **CC0 1.0 Universal** (public domain).

If you replace these files with higher-quality recordings, keep the filenames
so the `TraditionsSection` component keeps working without changes.
